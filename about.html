Girlene's was started by four brothers of Trinidadian descent. With recipes that have been in the family for more than three generations, Girlene's Caribbean & Soul Cuisine features some of the most authentic Caribbean cuisine in the D.C. area. From the Brown Stew Chicken, Candied Yams, Macaroni & Cheese and Oxtail to a large assortment of cakes and pies, at Girlene's, everything is home made and farm fresh.



The delicious meals are carefully prepared with the freshest ingredients and you can have an amazing dining experience without breaking the bank. No need to worry about sacrificing your taste buds for healthy options. Girlene's provides extraordinary vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free entrees sure to satisfy all of your dietary needs.



Do yourself a favor and come see for yourself what everyone is talking about.
